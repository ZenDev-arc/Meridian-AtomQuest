# Meridian — Goal Setting & Tracking Portal
### Complete Product Plan

---

## 1. What we are building

Meridian is a web-based Goal Setting and Tracking Portal — an internal performance management tool that digitises the complete lifecycle of employee goals across a financial year. It replaces spreadsheets, email chains, and offline review cycles with a structured, role-aware, audit-ready system.

The portal supports three user roles (Employee, Manager, Admin), enforces quarterly check-in windows, computes progress scores automatically based on the unit of measurement, and gives HR real-time visibility into completion rates across the organisation.

The name Meridian reflects the idea of a fixed reference line — the point from which all progress is measured.
